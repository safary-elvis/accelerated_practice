Introduction

The dataset analysed in this report provides information about 395 deaths caused by motorcycle accidents in Medellín, a Colombian city, from 2012 to 2015. The data is sourced from medical and police certificates and can be accessed here.

Age-sex distribution of motorcylce deaths

In this analysis, we looked at the number of deaths per age and sex group.

The age-sex group with the most fatal accidents was Males aged 20-24, with 80 deaths.
